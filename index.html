How to find your IDs

Your FPL Entry ID

  1. Open your team on the FPL website.
  2. Go to your Points or History page. The URL looks like:
https://fantasy.premierleague.com/entry/1234567/history
  1. The number in red is your Entry ID. You can paste the full URL here — we’ll auto-extract it.

Classic League ID

  1. Open the league table on the FPL website.
  2. The URL looks like:
https://fantasy.premierleague.com/leagues/classic/987654/standings
  1. The number in red is the League ID. You can paste multiple URLs or IDs, comma-separated.
